How Artificial Intelligence will affect our life

Posted on 2018-09-20 | In Artificial Intelligence |

Today I will take you through a very interesting topic that is Artificial Intelligence.

The field of artificial intelligence has been moving extremely quickly in the last few years. Do you know how AI will affect your life in the future?

If we want answer this question, we must know what is Artificial Intelligence first?

We are ready to understand what is artificial intelligence. Artificial Intelligence is nothing but the capability of the machine to imitate intelligent human behavior. AI is achieved by mimicking a human brain by understanding how it thinks and how it learns and work while trying to solve a problem. For example, a machine playing chess or a voice-activated software which helps you with various things in your phone.

Think about this instead of you doing all your work. You have a machine to finish it for you or it can do something which you thought it was not possible.

For example predicting the future like predicting earth quakes, tsunamis that preventive measures can be taken to save lives.

Chatbots, virtual personal assistants like Siri in iPhone Google assistant.
And believe me it is getting smarter day by day with deep learning /AI.

Self-driving cars it will be a blessing for elderly people disabled people who find it difficult to drive on their own. On top of that it can also avoid a lot of accidents that happen due to human error.

Google AI eye doctor, this is a recent initiative by Google where Google is working with an eye care chain to develop an AI software which can examine retina scans to identify a condition called diabetic retinopathy which can cause blindness.

Google AI eye doctor, Google deepman’s AI doctor can detect 50 eye diseases as accurately as a doctor.

AI music composer who thought that we can have an AI music composer using deep learning and maybe in the coming years even machines will start winning Grammys.

one of my favorites a dream reading machine. With so many unrealistic applications of AI and deep learning that we have seen so far, I was wondering that whether we can capture dreams in the form of a video or something and I wasn’t surprised to find out that this was tried in Japan a few years back on these test subjects and they were able to achieve close to 60% accuracy and this is amazing but I am not sure that whether people would want to be a test subject for this or not. Because it can reveal all your dreams.

We have to acknowledge also that AI is just a tool and it will stay a tool for the foreseeable future we don’t really have to worry about Skynet or terminator kinds of scenarios but what
is important is to understand that tools can be used in good ways and in bad ways in some ways AI is just like the internet or hammer or cars and you can use them as weapons or you can use them to transport sick people and it’s important for us to acknowledge that the tools are only as good as the people and the political systems that end up using them in fact if we use them well

In brief, AI will not replace biological intelligence yet it will enhance our lives it would enhance our future.
